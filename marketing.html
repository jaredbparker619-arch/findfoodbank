Who FindFoodBank Is For

FindFoodBank is designed for individuals and families experiencing food insecurity, as well as students, seniors, and community members who may not know where to find food assistance resources.

  • Individuals and families in need
  • College students
  • Seniors on fixed incomes
  • Community outreach workers

Why Choose FindFoodBank

  • Free and easy to use
  • Mobile-friendly and accessible
  • Reduces time spent searching for help
  • Improves awareness of local resources

Social Impact

By making food assistance information more accessible, FindFoodBank helps reduce barriers to food access and supports healthier, more resilient communities. The application demonstrates how technology can be used to address real social challenges.
